Both Milan clubs run global fanbases that a single-market content plan can't serve. Rather than adapting Italian assets outward, our desk builds calendar-native content in the market's own visual language: Islamic New Year artwork made for an Arabic-speaking audience, Hari Wayang Nasional content for Indonesia, a Spanish-language "Quién Soy" identity series — each built as if the club's content team sat in that market.

The difference shows in the numbers, but it shows first in the comments: fans responding in their own language, to content that doesn't read like an export.

### The result

- A single Arabic-language post passed 22,000+ reactions
- Localised artwork for the Indonesian market reached 14,200+ views
