A player interview loses something in a machine translation — tone, humour, the specific phrase a fan will quote back for a season. Native-speaking editors catch what's worth keeping and know how to say it in the audience's own voice.

### What's included

- Player-led storytelling built around what a market actually cares about
- Interview and sound-on-tape localisation
- Voice-over and subtitling by native speakers, not automated dubbing
