Fans notice when a reply sounds like it came from a script. Our community team moderates and converses in the fan's own language and tone, around the clock, with a clear escalation path when something needs a club's attention.

### What's included

- 24/7 multilingual moderation across every channel
- Fan conversation and care that sounds like a person, not a policy
- Escalation and crisis cover when matchday goes off-script
