One fixture. Nine markets. Roughly ten pieces of native content per market — live commentary, social posts, reaction packages, artwork — and the same ninety minutes as the match itself to publish most of it.

This is the operational core of what we do: not producing a lot of content, but producing the right content, in the right language, before the moment it responds to has passed.

### How the desk is structured

Each market has an editor who owns the fixture end to end, backed by a shared production team for artwork and video. Nobody is translating anybody else's post — every market's editor is briefed on the match and writes their own.
