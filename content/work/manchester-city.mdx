Manchester City came to us with a narrow brief in the 2018/19 season: Russian-language social coverage for matchdays. It was meant to fill a gap, not start a partnership.

Three years later, the same desk was running French web and social end to end — not translating what the English team produced, but publishing original content built for the French-speaking audience from the ground up.

Seven years on, both markets are still run by the same desk, with editors who know the fanbase, the rivalries and the running jokes better than a rotating vendor ever could.

### The result

- Two markets run fully end-to-end, from web to social to community
- Seven years of continuous partnership, with the same core editors throughout
